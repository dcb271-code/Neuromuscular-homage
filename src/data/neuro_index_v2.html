Caveats: Inclusion decisions are based on knowledge of the WashU site's structure as of early 2025 and may not reflect recent additions or reorganizations. A small number of borderline entries (genes with brief but named subsections) may have been conservatively excluded. Verification against the primary source is always recommended. Clinical decisions should never rely on this index alone — cross-reference OMIM, ClinVar, and Orphanet as appropriate.
